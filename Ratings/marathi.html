TIMEPASS

Timepass is a 2014 Indian Marathi language film. It is a story about teenage love set up in 90's between Dagadu (Prathamesh Parab) and Prajakta (Ketaki Mategaonkar), also starring Bhalchandra Kadam and Vaibhav Mangle.(170 mins)
Director: Ravi Jadhav
Stars: Prathamesh Parab, Ketaki Mategaonkar, Vaibhav Mangle, Bhalchandra Kadam

LAI BHARI

Lai Bhaari is a 2014 Marathi action,drama directed by Nishikant Kamat. The film marks the debut of Riteish Deshmukh in Marathi cinema, while Salman Khan and Genelia D'Souza also feature in cameo appearances.(150 mins)
Director: Nishikant Kamat
Stars: Riteish Deshmukh, Sharad Kelkar, Uday Tikekar, Tanvi Azmi, Radhika Apte

MUMBAI PUNE MUMBAI

Mumbai-Pune-Mumbai is an Indian Marathi language romantic drama film released on 11 June 2010. The movie is based on a love story between a boy and girl from different cities of Pune and Mumbai respectively and how their competitive spirits get them together.(147 mins.)
